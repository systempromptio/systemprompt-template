# Content Writing Skill

This skill enables the AI assistant to help with various writing and editing tasks.

## Capabilities

- Write blog posts and articles
- Edit and improve existing text
- Create documentation
- Draft emails and messages
- Summarize content
- Adapt tone and style

## Content Types

This skill can help with:

- **Blog posts**: Informative articles for your blog
- **Documentation**: Technical guides and references
- **Marketing copy**: Product descriptions and promotional content
- **Emails**: Professional and personal correspondence
- **Social media**: Posts and captions
- **General writing**: Any text-based content

## Writing Guidelines

When creating content:

1. **Understand the audience** - Tailor language and complexity appropriately
2. **Maintain consistency** - Use consistent tone, style, and formatting
3. **Structure clearly** - Use headings, lists, and paragraphs effectively
4. **Be concise** - Remove unnecessary words while preserving meaning
5. **Proofread** - Check for errors and clarity

## Editing Guidelines

When editing content:

1. **Preserve the author's voice** when possible
2. **Fix grammar and spelling** errors
3. **Improve clarity** without changing meaning
4. **Enhance flow** between sentences and paragraphs
5. **Suggest improvements** rather than rewriting entirely
