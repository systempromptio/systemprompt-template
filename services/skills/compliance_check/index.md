## When to Use

Invoke when changes affect data handling, user privacy, logging, or audit trail requirements.

## Checks

1. **Data Classification** — Is data categorized (public, internal, confidential, restricted)? Are handling rules followed for each level?
2. **Audit Trail** — Are significant actions logged with who, what, when, and where? Are logs tamper-resistant?
3. **Data Retention** — Are retention policies enforced? Is data deleted when required?
4. **Access Logging** — Are access attempts (success and failure) recorded?
5. **Encryption** — Is data encrypted at rest and in transit? Are key management practices followed?

## Output Format

For each check:

### [Check Name] — [COMPLIANT / NON-COMPLIANT / NOT APPLICABLE]
**Findings:** What was observed
**Requirement:** The standard or policy being checked
**Remediation:** Steps to achieve compliance (if non-compliant)
