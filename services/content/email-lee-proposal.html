What the Platform Does

The platform is a single self-hosted Rust binary backed by PostgreSQL. It provides a centralised Control Center for governing AI usage across an organisation: role-based access control, audit trails on every tool call and session, skills and plugin management via a built-in marketplace, and full analytics and observability.

The platform is licensed under the Business Source License 1.1. It is source-available, fully auditable, with no telemetry and no phone-home.
